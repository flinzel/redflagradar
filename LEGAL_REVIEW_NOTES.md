## Why exact subscription prices are not hardcoded

The pages intentionally state that exact prices, durations, trials, renewal and cancellation details are displayed by Apple in the purchase flow and configured in App Store Connect. This avoids legal risk if price tiers, currencies or trial availability differ by country or change later.
