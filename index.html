Before you reply, get clear.

Red Flag Radar Check is a calm, local-first iOS app for reflecting on unclear signals, boundaries, replies and next steps. In the installed app, the shorter name Red Flag Radar may be used. It is designed to support self-reflection, not to diagnose, judge or replace professional help.

Privacy-first setup: No account, no provider cloud sync, no analytics SDK, no crash SDK and no advertising tracking in the current version.
