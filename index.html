Before you reply, get clear.

RedFlag Radar is a calm, local-first iOS app for reflecting on unclear signals, boundaries, replies and next steps. It is designed to support self-reflection, not to diagnose, judge or replace professional help.

Privacy-first setup: No account, no provider cloud sync, no analytics SDK, no crash SDK and no advertising tracking in the current version.
