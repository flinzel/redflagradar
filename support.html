Support

Need help with Red Flag Radar Check, shown in the installed app as Red Flag Radar, subscriptions or a technical issue? Contact support by email.

F.Kintzel@gmail.com

Please include

  • Your device model.
  • Your iOS version.
  • The app version/build if visible.
  • A short description of the issue.
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible.

Subscriptions

Purchases, subscriptions, cancellations, refunds and billing are handled by Apple. For billing issues, Apple Support or your Apple account subscription settings may be the right place to start.

Privacy-friendly support

Please do not send unnecessary private details, names, screenshots with sensitive conversations or identifying information about other people. A short technical description is usually enough.

Not for emergencies

Support email is not monitored as an emergency channel. If you or another person may be in danger, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional immediately.
